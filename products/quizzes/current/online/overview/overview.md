## Quizzes

Interactive guided shopping — a series of questions that narrow down product recommendations. Useful for complex purchases where users don't know exactly what they want (skincare, supplements, gifts).

| Adaptive branching | Skipping irrelevant questions based on previous answers | TODO |

### ML Opportunities

- **Adaptive question order:** ask the most informative question next (information gain maximization)
- **Answer → embedding:** map quiz answers to user preference embedding (same space as personalization)
- **Auto-generation:** generate quiz questions from product attribute distributions
- **Result confidence:** show confidence score for each recommendation
