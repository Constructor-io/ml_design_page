## Additional Sources

The architecture allows adding new streams without changing ranking logic.

- **Offline Purchases** — CRM/POS data from customer (enriches user profile, co-purchase graph)
- **Sponsored Listings** — promoted items with relevance gate and reserved quota
- **Relevant Articles** — guides, reviews for informational queries
