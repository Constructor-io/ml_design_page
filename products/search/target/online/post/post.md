## Post-processing

Business rules after ML ranking. Does not re-rank — only presentation and business constraints.

- **Merchandising:** pin item to slot, boost/bury brand or category
- **Diversity:** max N same brand/category in top-K, minimal displacement from ML order
- **Ad slotting:** sponsored items in reserved positions
